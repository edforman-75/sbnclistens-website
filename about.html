üéØ The Goal

Identify and fix 7-8 critical pages on the SBNC website before term ends.

We're using abbreviated Design Thinking to work fast and focus on what matters most to our members.

‚ú® Pre-Work is Optional

Totally optional! If you can do interviews, great. If not, just come to the workshop on Nov 18th.

Pre-work helps us arrive knowing what to fix, but your participation at the workshop is what matters most.

What is Pre-Work?

Optional pre-work involves conducting 2-3 friendly 15-minute conversations with people who've used the SBNC website. It's actually fun - people enjoy sharing their experiences!

The Simple 4-Step Process:

  1. Read the guides (30 min) - Learn how to conduct great interviews
  2. Find 2-3 people - Recent members, inactive members, or former members
  3. Conduct interviews (15 min each) - Just press record and have a conversation
  4. Submit online (3 min each) - Upload recording and answer 3 quick questions

Total time: 1-2 hours

  • 30 min: Reading guides
  • 45 min: Three 15-min interviews
  • 9 min: Submitting three recordings

Flexible timing - do it when it works for you!

What You'll Accomplish:

Your interviews will reveal exactly which pages frustrate members and why. At the Nov 18th workshop, we'll show everyone what we learned and use it to decide which 7-8 pages to fix first.

Timeline

Technology team reviews all interview recordings and identifies patterns and pain points.

Collaborative workshop at Ed & Jan's home. Review findings, prioritize 7-8 pages, design solutions together.
