Key Insights from Examples

What Works Well in Wild Apricot

  • Clean navigation: Simple, clear menu structures (5-7 main items)
  • Visual hierarchy: Good use of white space and typography
  • Featured content: Homepage highlighting upcoming events and key actions
  • Mobile-first: Responsive designs that prioritize mobile experience
  • Custom branding: Consistent colors, fonts, and imagery throughout

Common Pitfalls to Avoid

  • Too many menu items: Navigation with 10+ items overwhelms users
  • Cluttered homepages: Trying to show everything at once
  • Inconsistent styling: Different fonts/colors on different pages
  • Poor mobile experience: Desktop-only designs that break on phones
  • Hidden member login: Making it hard to find the login button
