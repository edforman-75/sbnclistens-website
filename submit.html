ðŸ¤” Common Questions

Can I submit multiple interviews?

Absolutely! Submit the form once for each interview you conduct. If you do 3 interviews, you'll submit the form 3 times (once per recording).

What if my file is too large?

Most 15-minute recordings are under 15 MB, which uploads fine. If you have issues, email the file to technology@sbnewcomers.org instead.

What if I forgot to record part of the interview?

No problem! Submit what you have and note it in the "Anything we should know?" section. Partial interviews are still valuable!

Do I need to transcribe the interview?

Nope! Just upload the audio. We handle all transcription and analysis. Your job is done after clicking submit!

What if I can't submit by Nov 15th?

Reach out to us! We prefer Nov 15th to allow analysis time, but we can work with you if you need a bit more time.
