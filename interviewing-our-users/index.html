Interviewing Our Users

This section contains all the guides and templates you need to conduct effective user interviews using Design Thinking principles. These resources help volunteers gather valuable feedback from SBNC members about their website experience.

What's in This Section

This folder contains comprehensive guides for conducting user research interviews. Whether you're new to interviewing or an experienced facilitator, these documents will help you gather meaningful insights from SBNC members about their website experience.

New to user interviews? Start with the START HERE Guide below, then use the Interview Cheat Sheet during your conversations.

Typical Interview Process

  1. Read the START HERE Guide - Get familiar with the project goals and interview approach (30 minutes)
  2. Find 2-3 participants - Recent members, inactive members, or former members work great. Use the email template to invite them.
  3. Set up recording - Follow the Recording Guide to prepare your device before the call
  4. Conduct the interview - Keep the Interview Cheat Sheet open during your 15-minute conversation
  5. Submit your recording - Upload the audio file and answer 3 quick questions (3 minutes per interview)
