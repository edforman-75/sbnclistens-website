Welcome to SBNC Listens!

We're listening to our members to improve the website. You're helping prepare for the November 18th workshop where we'll fix 7-8 critical pages before term ends.

What is This?

Optional pre-work: Conduct 2-3 friendly 15-minute interviews with people who've used the SBNC website. It's actually fun - people enjoy sharing their experiences!

The Simple Process

  1. Read the guides (30 min) - You're doing this now!
  2. Check who's already being interviewed - View the tracking list to avoid duplicates, then register your interviewees
  3. Find 2-3 people - Recent members, inactive members, former members (who aren't already on the list!)
  4. Record conversations (15 min each) - Just press record and chat
  5. Submit online (3 min each) - Drag, drop, done

Who Should You Talk To?

Anyone who's encountered the SBNC website! Try to get variety:

  • Someone thinking about joining
  • Recent members (joined in last 6-12 months)
  • Active current members
  • CRITICAL: Inactive current members (Why aren't they engaged?)
  • Former members (Why did they leave?)
  • Mix of ages

IMPORTANT: Don't just interview super-engaged members!

The most valuable insights come from:

  • People who joined but aren't very active
  • People who attended once and didn't return
  • People who browse website but rarely attend
  • Former members who let membership lapse
  • People who considered joining but didn't

Why? Highly engaged members figured out workarounds - they won't tell us what's broken!
