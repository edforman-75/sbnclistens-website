# SBNC Website User Research
## Presentation-Ready Analysis | 5 Interviews
*Generated: November 16, 2025*

---

## Executive Summary

**Research Goal:** Understand user experience with SBNC website to inform redesign

**Methodology:** Qualitative user interviews (5 participants)

**Key Finding:** Website creates unnecessary friction in finding, navigating, and managing events


---

## Slide 1: Top 5 Critical Issues

### Pain Points Ranked by Frequency


**1. Navigation/Backtracking** (4 mentions)
   - *"I feel like I'm, I have to go back a lot more steps that way than with the app...."*
   - Impact: Increases the time and effort required to find events, leading to frustration.

**2. Event Cancellation** (3 mentions)
   - *"There should just be a way to automate that somehow...."*
   - Impact: Creates unnecessary effort and potential for miscommunication.

**3. Search Functionality** (2 mentions)
   - *"I think it would be easier if you could pick whatever committee that you think or, you know, I don't..."*
   - Impact: Makes finding relevant events tedious and inefficient.

**4. Alumni Directory** (2 mentions)
   - *"How do they find each other after they're done?..."*
   - Impact: Limits networking opportunities with alumni.

## Slide 3: What Users Want to Accomplish

### Top Functional Jobs

1. When I want to join a social club, I want to sign up easily, so I can become a member and engage with the community.

2. When I want to contact committee members, I want to easily access the member directory, so I can connect with them.

3. When I want to find events to attend, I want to easily navigate the website or app, so I can plan my participation.

4. When I am trying to find events on the website, I want to easily navigate and search for events by category or keyword, so I can efficiently find what I am interested in.

5. When I am looking for events to participate in, I want to easily find and sign up for them, so I can stay engaged and enjoy the activities.

## Slide 6: Recommendations

### Priority Fixes


**HIGH PRIORITY:**

6. Streamline payment process (reduce CAPTCHA friction)
