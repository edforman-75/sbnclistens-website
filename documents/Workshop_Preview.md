## What We'll Reveal

### The Patterns You Helped Discover
We'll show you themes that emerged across ALL the interviews. You'll see how that one thing someone told you showed up as a major pattern across multiple conversations.

### Your Specific Contributions
We'll highlight moments from YOUR interviews that led to key insights. You'll recognize quotes and stories from the people you talked to (anonymized, of course).

### Surprising "Aha!" Moments  
There are always insights that nobody expected. We'll reveal the hidden truths that only emerged when we analyzed all the conversations together.

### Real Recommendations
Based on what we learned from your interviews, we'll present actual, actionable recommendations for improving the SBNC website. These aren't guesses - they're directly based on what real users told you.

## Why It's Fun

**You'll experience the "reveal"** - Like watching a mystery solved, you'll see how individual stories connect into a bigger picture.

**You'll see your impact** - "Wait, that one comment Maria made to me became a whole theme?"

**You'll learn a new skill** - Understanding how Design Thinking works by experiencing it firsthand.

**You'll get the context** - All those questions in the interview guide? You'll understand WHY they matter.

## What You'll Learn About Design Thinking

### During the Interviews (Your Part)
- **Empathize**: You learned to really listen without judging
- **Ask**: You practiced asking "why" to go deeper
- **Observe**: You noticed emotions and patterns as they emerged

### At the Workshop (What We'll Show You)
- **Define**: How we turned stories into problem statements
- **Insight**: How patterns revealed underlying needs
- **Solutions**: How real user feedback leads to actual improvements

By experiencing Step 1 (Empathize) hands-on and then seeing Steps 2-3 (Define & Insight) in action, you'll understand the entire Design Thinking approach - not from a textbook, but from doing it!

---

## A Typical Workshop Flow

1. **Welcome & Thank You** (5 min)
   - We celebrate your contributions!

2. **What We Heard** (20 min)
   - We share the major themes and patterns
   - Real (anonymized) quotes from your interviews
   - Surprising discoveries nobody expected

3. **How We Got There** (15 min)
   - We show you our analysis process
   - You'll see how we coded the transcripts
   - How patterns emerged from individual stories

4. **The Insights** (15 min)
   - The "aha!" moments that changed our understanding
   - What this means for the SBNC website
   - Which problems are most critical to solve

5. **Recommendations** (15 min)
   - Specific improvements we're proposing
   - Why each recommendation addresses real user needs
   - How your interviews directly informed these ideas

6. **Q&A & Discussion** (15 min)
   - Your reactions and thoughts
   - Questions about what you learned
   - Next steps for implementing improvements

## Why This Approach Works

Traditional methods: Survey 100 people, get superficial answers, guess at solutions.

Design Thinking: Talk deeply with 10-15 people, understand their real experiences, design solutions that actually address their needs.

**Your interviews are the foundation for everything.** Without your careful listening and curious questions, we'd just be guessing. With them, we have real insights that lead to real improvements.
