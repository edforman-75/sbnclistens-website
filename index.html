About SBNC Listens

SBNC Listens is a user research initiative using Design Thinking methodology to improve the Santa Barbara Newcomers Club website. Through member interviews and collaborative workshops, we're identifying pain points and creating solutions that truly meet member needs.

The project follows a structured approach:

  1. Research - Conduct user interviews to understand member experiences and needs
  2. Analysis - Synthesize findings to identify patterns, pain points, and opportunities
  3. Prioritization - Collaboratively determine which improvements will have the biggest impact
  4. Design - Create solutions through workshops and task force work
  5. Implementation - Roll out improvements and gather feedback
